Our Objectives

Four complementary goals drive INCREASE4MED towards a resilient Mediterranean.

Strategic Goals

Click on each objective to learn more about how we build resilience across ecological, technological and social dimensions.

Objective 1: Sustainable Water & Soil Management

Implement multiple, regionally adapted nature‑based solutions to boost water and soil sustainability.

  • Establish comprehensive baseline datasets for each pilot site.
  • Deploy tailored portfolios of NbS including buffer strips, terracing and ecological pest management.
  • Compare business‑as‑usual and NbS scenarios using hydrological models.
  • Analyse synergistic and antagonistic effects on water use, soil moisture and ecosystem services.
  • Publish raw and processed data in open repositories following FAIR principles.
Objective 2: Digital NbS Planner

Develop an open‑source decision‑support tool to optimise nature‑based solutions.

  • Create a user‑friendly, multi‑criteria planner that reaches Technology Readiness Level 7.
  • Validate the planner with field data and provide cost–benefit analyses for NbS portfolios.
  • Engage stakeholders through workshops and training, ensuring high user satisfaction.
  • Release the tool’s source code and documentation under an open licence for long‑term accessibility.
Objective 3: Socio‑Economic & Governance

Enhance social acceptance and develop innovative governance and financing models.

  • Identify and engage stakeholders including farmers, communities, authorities and NGOs.
  • Assess social acceptance and behavioural change via surveys and focus groups.
  • Pilot community‑based financing schemes such as NbS‑as‑a‑Service or Payments for Ecosystem Services.
  • Provide training for at least 200 participants with a strong focus on women and youth.
  • Ensure ongoing ethical and legal compliance.
Objective 4: Monitoring & Policy Integration

Monitor impacts, influence policies and secure sustainability for future generations.

  • Regularly measure environmental, economic and social indicators across pilots.
  • Publish results in open data repositories and develop policy briefs and roadmaps.
  • Integrate project outputs into EU and national strategies while ensuring ethical compliance.
  • Establish dissemination and communication mechanisms for long‑term collaboration and open data.
