Building Resilience for the Mediterranean

INCREASE4MED brings together nature‑based solutions, science and communities across six Mediterranean countries to create sustainable water and land management.

About the Project

The Mediterranean region is warming faster than the global average, putting ecosystems, agriculture and local communities under pressure. INCREASE4MED is an international, EU‑funded project that integrates tailored nature‑based solutions, innovative digital tools and multi‑actor governance to boost resilience and restore ecological balance across diverse pilot regions.

6 Countries

Our pilot sites span Türkiye, Greece, Italy, Jordan, Morocco and Spain, representing the rich diversity of the Mediterranean basin.

7 Pilots

Each pilot region tests a unique portfolio of nature‑based solutions tailored to local challenges in water, soil and ecosystems.

9 Partners

A multidisciplinary consortium of universities, research institutes, public bodies and SMEs collaborate to deliver innovative outcomes.

4 Objectives

Increasing sustainable water and soil management, developing decision‑support tools, enhancing socio‑economic acceptance, and scaling up policy integration.
