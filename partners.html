INCREASE4MED unites leading institutions across Europe, the Mediterranean and beyond.

Consortium

Our multidisciplinary consortium comprises universities, research institutes, public bodies and SMEs. Together, we bring expertise from agronomy, hydrology, socio‑economics and environmental technology to address the Mediterranean’s pressing challenges.
